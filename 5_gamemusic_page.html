My Favorite Game

Wang Chuan Dream

         I really enjoy the game Wang Chuan Dream.It's a fantasy world based on Chinese history, where historical figures from various professions are gathered. In this world, we can experience the collision of thoughts and history from different periods. The fun gameplay is so engaging that I often lose track of time, and it also has educational value. Moreover, there's a music project with the same name, so I can listen to beautiful music while playing the game, which I find very satisfying.
